HedgeSlammer

This page illustrates how applying a HedgeSlammer affects edge-cubies and corner-cubies. The HedgeSlammer need not be restricted to a cube. It affects the pieces of other twisty-puzzles in a similar manner.

HedgeSlammer

The HedgeSlammer.
Step Desc Alg
1 Turn the Front face clockwise F
2 Turn the Right face counter-clockwise R'
3 Turn the Front face counter-clockwise F'
4 Turn the Right face clockwise R

The example above used the Front and Right face, but any two adjacent faces can be used. Using the Up face and the Back face are shown below, but notice that no matter which two faces are used, the cube can always be re-oriented so that the Front and Right faces are used.

HedgeSlammer using the Up and Back faces.

1 Turn the Up face clockwise U
2 Turn the Back face counter-clockwise B'
3 Turn the Up face counter-clockwise U'
4 Turn the Back face clockwise B

It is important to know how a HedgeSlammer affects the cube. The effects are illustrated below.

Exactly seven cubies are affected: four corner-cubies and three edge-cubies.
Two diagonal corner-cubies in the top layer swap positions.
Two corner-cubies swap places between the top and bottom layers.
Three edge-cubies move one step counter-clockwise around a common corner-cubie. (Compare the starting and ending positions of the edge-cubies.)
The edge-cubie on the top face 'flips'. (The edge-cubie starts and ends on the top face and its ending orientation is 'flipped' compared to its starting orientation.)
The edge-cubie on the front face 'rotates' clockwise. (The edge-cubie starts and ends on the front face and its ending position is 'rotated' about the green center cubie compared to its starting position.)
The edge-cubie on the right face 'rotates' clockwise. (The edge-cubie starts and ends on the right face and its ending position is 'rotated' about the red center cubie compared to its starting position.)
Applying HedgeSlammer twice, twists the corner-cubie in the bottom layer. (It also twists other corner-cubies, but they can be ignored.)
Step Desc Alg
1-4 HedgeSlammer F R' F' R
5-8 HedgeSlammer F R' F' R
Applying HedgeSlammer six times is the same as doing nothing. (All corner-cubies and edge-cubies are returned to their starting positions and orientations.)
